## Appendix B: Interview Guide

### Standard Interview Questions (Sample)

**CISO / Security Leadership:**
1. How is AI security governance structured in your organization?
2. What AI-specific security policies exist?
3. How do you measure AI security maturity?
4. What are your top AI security concerns?

**AI/ML Platform Lead:**
1. What AI/ML systems are deployed in production?
2. How are security requirements defined for AI systems?
3. What controls exist for model deployment?
4. How is human oversight implemented?

**Compliance Officer:**
1. What regulatory requirements apply to your AI systems?
2. How do you document AI governance for auditors?
3. What compliance gaps concern you most?
