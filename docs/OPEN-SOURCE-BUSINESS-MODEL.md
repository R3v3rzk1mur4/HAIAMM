## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Competitors Offering Free Assessments

**Mitigation:**
- Quality differentiation (official certification vs. free)
- Brand value ("HAIAMM Certified" vs. "used HAIAMM")
- Benchmarking data access (only through official program)
- Insurance/regulatory recognition (only official certifications)

### Risk 2: Slow Paid Conversion

**Mitigation:**
- Freemium platform (limited free tier, paid advanced features)
- Value-added services (consulting, training)
- Multiple revenue streams (not just certifications)
- Partner ecosystem (scale through others)

### Risk 3: Framework Fork/Competition

**Mitigation:**
- CC BY-SA license (derivatives must be open source too)
- Network effects (original HAIAMM has most users)
- Trademark protection ("HAIAMM" name)
- Community governance (trusted stewardship)
