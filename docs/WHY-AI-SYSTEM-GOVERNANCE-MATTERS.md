## What HAIAMM Assesses: The 4 Functions √ó 6 Domains Model

### The Four Business Functions

**1. GOVERNANCE - "How do you govern AI implementations?"**
- Strategy & Metrics: Alignment with business objectives
- Policy & Compliance: Policies, auditability, explainability
- Education & Guidance: Training, oversight, human judgment

**2. BUILDING - "How do you build AI systems responsibly?"**
- Threat Assessment: AI-specific risks (prompt injection, bias, etc.)
- Security Requirements: Safety and security requirements
- Secure Architecture: Design with appropriate controls

**3. VERIFICATION - "How do you verify AI works correctly?"**
- Design Review: Review before deployment
- Code Review: Prompts, configurations, generated outputs
- Security Testing: Testing for vulnerabilities and failures

**4. OPERATIONS - "How do you operate AI in production?"**
- Environment Hardening: Runtime security
- Monitoring & Logging: Decision audit trails
- Issue Management: Handling errors and failures

### The Six Operational Domains

1. **Software**: AI in applications, AI-generated code
2. **Infrastructure**: Deployment environments, scaling
3. **Endpoints**: User interfaces, APIs, integration points
4. **Data**: Training data, privacy, quality, governance
5. **Processes**: Business workflows involving AI
6. **Vendors**: Third-party AI services, supply chain
