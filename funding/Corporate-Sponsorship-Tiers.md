## Sponsorship Policies

### Logo Usage Guidelines
- Sponsors may use "HAIAMM Sponsor" badge on their materials
- HAIAMM may use sponsor logos for recognition purposes
- All logo usage subject to mutual approval
- Sponsorship does not imply endorsement of sponsor's products

### Conflict of Interest
- Sponsors cannot influence assessment outcomes
- Sponsorship status not disclosed during certifications
- Technical Advisory Board members recuse from competitive discussions
- All governance decisions made transparently

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with 90 days notice
- Pro-rated refunds for annual commitments terminated early
- Recognition materials updated within 30 days of termination
- No disparagement clause applies post-termination

## FAQ

**Q: Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?**
A: Depends on your jurisdiction and HAIAMM's organizational structure. We can provide documentation for your tax advisor.

**Q: Can I sponsor anonymously?**
A: Yes, anonymous sponsorship is available. Contact us for details.

**Q: What if I want to sponsor more than the Founding tier?**
A: We welcome conversations about custom partnership arrangements for major commitments.

**Q: Can sponsorship be paid monthly/quarterly?**
A: Bronze and Silver can be paid annually only. Gold and above can arrange quarterly payments.

**Q: Does sponsorship guarantee certification approval?**
A: No. Certification is based solely on assessment results. Sponsorship and certification are completely separate.

**Q: Can competitors both be sponsors?**
A: Yes. We welcome multiple sponsors from the same industry. All sponsors receive equal treatment within their tier.
